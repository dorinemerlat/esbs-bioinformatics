Ensembl

In the Ensembl genome browser, display the TRPM1 gene and configure the page to display small variants from the 1000 genomes project and phenotypes associated.

For that, open the Configure Page windows. - Open Variation: activate 1000 Genomes - All - short variants (SNPs and indels) and 1000 Genomes - All - common - short variants (SNPs and indels) (with Normal style). - Open Variation > Phenotype, disease and curated variants_: activate All phenotype-associated - short variants (SNPs and indels) (with Normal style).
